# Using Your Wallet

Learn how to send, receive, and manage your assets with WaaP.

## Receiving Funds

Your wallet address works like any other crypto wallet:

1. Click **Receive** or copy your address
2. Share the address with the sender
3. Funds appear once the transaction confirms

## Sending Funds

1. Click **Send**
2. Enter the recipient's address
3. Enter the amount
4. Review the transaction details
5. Confirm

## Supported Chains

WaaP works across multiple blockchains:

- **EVM Chains** — Ethereum, Optimism, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, and more
- **Sui** — Full support with Sui Wallet Standard
- **More coming** — Bitcoin, Solana

## Transaction History

View all your past transactions in the wallet interface. Each transaction shows:
- Amount and token
- Recipient or sender address
- Status (pending, confirmed, failed)
- Date and time
